About Us

Book HSRP https://www.bookmyhsrpr.com/ is an authorized provider of High Security Registration Plates (HSRP), operating under government licenses and certifications. We are committed to delivering genuine, compliant HSRP plates that meet all regulatory standards. Our HSRP plates are manufactured according to strict quality standards and feature advanced security elements including:

  • Chromium-based hologram
  • Laser-branded permanent identification number
  • Non-removable snap-lock fitting system
  • Standardized fonts and specifications
We maintain full compliance with:
  • Motor Vehicles Act regulations
  • State Transport Authority guidelines
  • Government mandated HSRP specifications
